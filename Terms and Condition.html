Introduction to the platform



Ankara

Ankara is a digital marketplace for fashion, aims at providing an express access to diversities of fashion arts, as well as to promote craftiness and creativity of artisans and fashion designers, ultimately making fashion arts available for general public and fashion enthusiasts to be purchased and get delivered at door step.

Art or Fashion Art

It is any items put up for sale on Ankara which can either be dresses, bags, shoes, slippers, bangles, earring, head wears and other fashion accessories.

Member

Anybody that registered or sign up has become a member of our platform (marketplace).

Seller

A seller is a fashion designers or artisan that create and sell or only sells fashions arts on our platform

Buyer

Anybody that has prospect of buying or has successfully purchased from the Ankara platform.

Obligations



  1. Ankara Oversees every orders received, by alerting the sellers whose items is purchased for execution of the order, track and report status to buyers till delivery all within delivery timeframe.
  2. Ankara check and regulates prices of items uploaded by sellers, flag or contact seller after the item assessment.
  3. Ankara does not guarantee sales for any sellers.
  4. Ankara has no control over product or its quality control, but prioritize customer satisfaction and compensates sellers with positive feedback by giving them selling exposure.
  5. Ankara does not guarantee buyers refund of money, in the case of delivery, if the order is not delivered and Ankara will see to the order completion upon contacting support.
  6. Ankara sees to how every item purchased on the platform is delivered to the appropriate address.
  7. Upon delivery, Ankara assumes anybody with the appropriate information provided when making order will be delivered to, in which Ankara will take picture evidence of delivery, and any case of impersonating will not be reckon.
  8. To become members, user must sign up and provide required information such as Username, Email, and Password.
  9. If buyers does not accept order on the platform after verified delivery, Ankara will automatically mark the order completed in three (3) days’ time.
  10. When any fashion items is ordered from a seller’s collection, lack of activeness of seller exceeding 24hours will make Ankara redirects the buyer’s order to another seller for the execution.
  11. To enroll as new seller, you must come through an existing seller, to serve as a form of recommendation of expertise, which comes with multi-level system of rewarding
  12. Ankara uses of cookies
  13. Pictures on the website are property of fashionists, so we're not responsible for copyright issues.
  14. Fashionists will be held responsible for defrauding a client with account disabling and restriction to sell on the platform.
  15. Clients are responsible for the % reward for fashionists based on satisfaction i.e. rating
  16. Fashionists get paid within 24/48 hours of withdrawal request
